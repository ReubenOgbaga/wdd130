Nigeria

Nigeria is located in West Africa, known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse languages, and vibrant cities. It is Africa's most populous country and boasts a large economy driven by oil and agriculture. The country is home to several ethnic groups and beautiful landscapes. The Capital of Nigeria is Abuja. The major ethnic groups in Nigeria include Igbo, Yoruba, and Hausa.
