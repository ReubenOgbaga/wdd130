Nigeria

Nigeria, officially known as the Federal Republic of Nigeria, is a country located in West Africa. It spans an area of approximately 923,769 square kilometers (356,669 square miles) and is home to over 230 million people, making it the most populous nation in Africa and the sixth most populous in the world14. The capital city is Abuja, while Lagos serves as the largest city and a major commercial hub.Nigeria features a diverse landscape that includes deserts, plains, swamps, mountains, and rainforests. It is characterized by significant river systems, notably the Niger River, which supports extensive agricultural activities.
